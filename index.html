About Me

As a Data Science graduate student at Northeastern University with a robust background in Mechanical Engineering, Research, and Teaching, I am passionate about leveraging data to solve complex problems, drive innovation, and make a lasting impact on people's lives. With experience as a Graduate Teaching Assistant in Algorithms and a successful internship as an Engineering Data Scientist at Veeco, I am well-equipped to tackle challenges in the data science field.

My diverse experience includes developing advanced machine learning models for semiconductor manufacturing, conducting research on gun violence and patient mortality, and securing $2.1 million in research funding. Proficient in Python, R, MATLAB, SQL, PyTorch, and TensorFlow, I have extensive experience in machine learning, data analysis, statistical modeling, and software development. My interdisciplinary skills allow me to approach challenges from different angles and deliver innovative, data-driven solutions.

Seeking full-time opportunities starting Summer 2025, my goal is to apply my expertise in a dynamic data science role, utilizing the power of data to optimize processes, enhance decision-making, and contribute positively to society. With a track record of published research and practical industry experience, I am ready to make significant contributions in the field of data science.
